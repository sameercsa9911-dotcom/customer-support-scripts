# Customer Complaint Handling Guide

## Step 1: Listen Carefully
Always allow the customer to explain their issue without interruption.

## Step 2: Show Empathy
Acknowledge the issue and apologize sincerely for the inconvenience.

## Step 3: Identify the Problem
Ask relevant questions to clearly understand the root cause.

## Step 4: Provide a Solution
Offer the best possible solution or escalate the issue if required.

## Step 5: Confirm Resolution
Ensure the customer is satisfied before closing the case.

## Step 6: Close Politely
Thank the customer for their patience and time.
